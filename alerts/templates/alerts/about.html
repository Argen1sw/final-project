Our Concept

Welcome to EnviroAlerts: a community-driven, open source platform dedicated to empowering underserved communities. We believe that everyone deserves access to real-time environmental insights without the burden of costly infrastructure.

What is EnviroAlerts?

Our platform allows you to visualize, publish, and manage a variety of environmental alerts through an intuitive web interface. Whether you are tracking local river water levels, monitoring storm patterns, or alerting neighbors to wildfires, EnviroAlerts bridges the gap between cutting-edge IoT integration and grassroots knowledge.

Explore our API documentation to learn how to integrate low-cost IoT devices with our system.

Why EnviroAlerts?

By leveraging affordable IoT solutions and crowdsourced data, EnviroAlerts transforms local observations into a powerful, worldwide alert system. Our flexible design enables you to add new alert types, contribute your own insights, and collaborate with others.

Whether you are a concerned citizen, an environmental activist, or a local authority, our platform empowers you to take action.

Join Us

Our mission is simple: empower communities, promote accessibility, and foster a sustainable model of collective vigilance. We are building a resilient future where every voice counts and no community is left behind in the fight against environmental hazards.
